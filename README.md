# Training Data Dashboard

A personal training case study visualizing 39 months of body composition and strength data (Oct 2022 – Jan 2026).

**Live:** [viizify.github.io/Training-data-dashboard](https://viizify.github.io/Training-data-dashboard)

---

## What it shows

- **Body weight** over time with phase shading
- **Body fat %** progression
- **Absolute 1RM** for bench press, squat, and deadlift
- **Relative strength** (lift / bodyweight) for all three lifts
- Key findings and phase-by-phase analysis

---

## Results at a glance

| Metric | Start | End | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Body weight | 115 kg | 84 kg | −31 kg |
| Body fat | 27% | 14% | −13 pp |
| Bench 1RM | 84 kg | 116 kg | +32 kg |
| Squat 1RM | 90 kg | 126 kg | +36 kg |
| Deadlift 1RM | 108 kg | 171 kg | +63 kg |

---

## Training phases

| Phase | Period | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| First cut | Oct 2022 – Aug 2023 | Initial weight loss |
| Second cut | Sep 2023 – Aug 2024 | Continued deficit |
| Third cut | Sep 2024 – May 2025 | Aggressive cut |
| Recomp | Jun 2025 – Jan 2026 | Maintain/build |
